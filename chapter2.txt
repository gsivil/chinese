Lǐ: Wǎng xiānsheng, zhè shì wǒ tàitai, Zhōu Déjīn.

Wáng: Lǐ tàitai, nín hǎo!

Lǐ: Wáng xiānsheng, nín hǎo! Rènshi nín, wǒ zhēn gāoxìng.

Wáng: Qǐng zuò qǐng zuò. Hē yì bēi jiǔ ba.

Lǐ: Xièxie, wǒ hē yì bēi.

Wáng: Lǐ tàitai ne?

Lǐ: Xièxie, wǒ bú huì hē jiǔ.

Wáng: Nàme, júzizhī hǎo ma?

Hǎo, xièxie nin.



Words list:

zhè(这）: this
shì （是）: to be
tàitai (太太）: Mrs, wife
nín（您）: you (polite form)
rènshi（认识）: to know, recognize
zhēn（真）: really, true, real
gāoxìng （高兴）: happy
yī(一）: one
bēi（杯）: cup
jiǔ（酒）: alcohol
ba（吧）: particle indicating suggestion
ne( 呢）: question particle
huì（会）: to know how, can, will.
júzi-zhī （桔子汁）: orange juice
ma(妈): question particle
